IT - Work

1. What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

IT professionals do a number of different tasks. They are the people who test, build, install, repair, or maintain the hardware and software associated with complex computer systems in one or more locations.

2. What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

IT professionals interact with many people from all fields

3. Where do the IT professionals spend most of their time?

People think that IT professionals spend most of their time on computer but it completely wrong because IT professionals only spend time on computer when they at work otherwise they will spend their time with their friend and family or learning to improve their programming skill.

4. What aspect of their position is most challenging?

The most difficult thing for IT is to solve bugs, face complex algorithms and keep up to date with the latest technology.
